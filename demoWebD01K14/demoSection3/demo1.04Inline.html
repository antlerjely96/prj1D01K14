Lau dai go o Pattaya

Nam tren mot doi dat nho o phia bac bien Pattaya, toa lau dai Prasat Sajjatham, tam dich la "Ngoi den chan ly" (The Sanctuary of Truth) khong nhung la mot phien ban hoan hao cua nghe thuat kien truc co ma con la mot minh chung cho su sang tao cua nen nghe thuat hien dai Thai Lan.

Lau dai Prasat Sajjatham duoc khoi cong vao ngay 13-8-1981, xuat phat tu y tuong cua mot nha ty phu Thai Lan - ong Lek Viriyaphan, chu nhan Cong ty Mo to Thonburi va cong ty bao hiem Viriya

"Ngoi den chan ly" co chieu cao 105m, chieu dai 100m, dien tich ben trong lau dai la 2.115m2, con dien tich toan bo khu vuc la 12,8ha. No duoc phan thanh 4 canh (chai) huong ra 4 phia, moi canh duoc thiet ke theo 1 kien truc khac nhau. Mot canh lam ta nho den kieu kien truc hoanh trang cua Angkor Wat, trong khi mot canh khac duoc thiet ke theo "mo tip" Trung Hoa
